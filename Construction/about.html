About Sher Constructions

Sher Construction & Convey Pvt. Ltd is a pioneer construction material supplying and construction equipment rental company situated at Kaushaltar, Bhaktapur. Sher Construction is managed by industry professionals with decades of experience and expertise in this field.
At Sher Construction & Convey Pvt. Ltd we rent different heavy types of equipment like dozer, cranes, trucks etc to meet your need for construction projects. Also Sher Construction undertakes construction projects of all scale.
We are known for our quality service and full customer satisfaction. Feel free to contact us anytime for your queries.
